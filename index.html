Instructions

  • You will be given a random number when you start the game.
  • There are 4 crystals which hold a random value between 1 and 12.
  • When you click on the Crystals the number values will be added to the total score.
  • Your objective is to match your score with the random number given to you at the start of the game.
  • You will not see the value of the crystal until you click on it.
  • The Crystals will reset when you start a new game
